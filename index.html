Investment Tips & Notes

  • Diversification is Key: While aggressive, ensure your portfolio is diversified across different sectors and asset classes to mitigate idiosyncratic risks.
  • Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs): Consider regular SIPs into mutual funds to average out purchase costs and benefit from compounding over the long term.
  • Rebalance Periodically: Review your portfolio at least once a year to ensure it aligns with your risk tolerance and financial goals. Rebalance if any asset class significantly deviates from its target allocation.
  • Long-Term Horizon: Aggressive portfolios are best suited for long-term investors (5+ years) who can ride out market volatility.
  • Emergency Fund: Always maintain an adequate emergency fund (6-12 months of expenses) in liquid assets, separate from your investment capital.
