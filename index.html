Tip: Diversification

Ensure your portfolio isn't overly concentrated in one asset class or sector. Combining Fixed Deposits with Mutual Funds provides a good balance between stability and growth, which is crucial for managing risk and achieving long-term goals.

Tip: SIPs (Systematic Investment Plans)

For mutual funds, investing through SIPs helps average out your purchase cost over time (Rupee Cost Averaging), reducing the impact of market volatility. Even small, regular contributions can compound significantly over the long term.

Tip: Hedges for Volatility

While this portfolio is low-risk, for future higher-equity allocations, consider assets like Gold ETFs or sovereign bonds as hedges during market downturns. They often perform well when equities decline, balancing overall portfolio risk.
