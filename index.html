Investment Strategies & Tips

Diversification is Key

While this portfolio is high-risk, diversification across stocks, mutual funds, and crypto helps spread risk. Avoid putting all your capital into a single asset class.

Power of SIPs

Consistent Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) help in rupee-cost averaging, reducing the impact of market volatility and allowing you to buy more units when prices are low.

Consider Hedges

For very high-risk portfolios, consider small allocations to instruments like gold or debt in the future to act as a hedge against equity market downturns, especially as your portfolio grows.

Regular Review

Review your portfolio at least quarterly to ensure it aligns with your goals, risk tolerance, and current market conditions. Rebalance if necessary to maintain target allocations.
