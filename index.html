Risk Corner: If Markets Crash

Estimated Shock Impact

  • Equities (50%): -35% to -50% drawdown in severe bear markets.
  • Crypto (10%): -60% to -80% possible due to high beta/liquidity risk.
  • Bonds (5%): -2% to -5% for quality debt; G-Secs may even gain if yields fall.
  • Gold (5%): 0% to +20% depending on flight-to-safety dynamics.
Portfolio-level worst-case drawdown could be roughly -25% to -35% over short windows.

Mitigations

  • Stagger entries via SIP/STP; rebalance annually to target weights.
  • Maintain 6–9 months of expenses in cash/ultra-short debt outside this portfolio.
  • Use SGB/Gold ETFs and quality bonds as volatility buffers.
  • Avoid over-concentration; the suggested stocks are diversified across autos, financials, and metals.
Why this mix?
  • Stocks: Primary growth engine with leaders like Eicher, Maruti, Bajaj Finance, SBI, JSW Steel.
  • Mutual Funds: Manager alpha and automatic diversification complement direct stocks.
  • Crypto: Capped 10% for asymmetric upside; split between BTC and ETH.
  • Bonds + Gold: Stabilizers to temper equity/crypto volatility and hedge macro shocks.
