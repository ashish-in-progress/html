Tips for Optimal Portfolio Management

  • Diversification is Key: Ensure your investments are spread across different asset classes (equities, debt, gold, crypto) to mitigate risk.

  • Long-Term Horizon: For high-risk, high-reward investments like equity and crypto, maintain a long-term perspective to ride out short-term fluctuations.
  • Emergency Fund: Always have an adequate emergency fund (3-6 months of expenses) before investing aggressively.
  • Consider Hedges: While not explicitly in this portfolio, consider assets like Gold or Debt as hedges during extreme market uncertainty.
