Risk Corner: Crash Impact Simulation

Understanding Potential Drawdowns

In a significant market downturn (e.g., -20% overall market crash), different asset classes react differently.

  • Stocks: Typically bear the brunt of market crashes, with potential significant percentage drops.
  • Mutual Funds: Also exposed to market volatility, but diversification within the fund can offer slight cushioning compared to individual stocks.
  • Crypto: Highly volatile; can see extreme drops, often correlating with or amplifying broader market sentiment.
  • Gold: Often acts as a safe haven, potentially seeing appreciation or minimal drops during crashes, providing a hedge.

Important Tips & Notes

Key Strategies for Your High-Growth Portfolio

  • Diversification is Key: While aggressive, this portfolio maintains diversification across stocks, mutual funds, crypto, and gold to spread risk and capture growth from different market segments.
  • Consistent SIPs: Continue your Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) diligently. This averages out your purchase cost and is crucial for long-term wealth creation, especially in volatile markets.
  • Hedge Against Volatility: Gold ETF acts as a traditional hedge against inflation and equity market downturns, providing stability when other assets might falter.
  • Rebalancing: Regularly review and rebalance your portfolio (e.g., annually) to maintain your target asset allocation. This prevents any single asset class from becoming over-represented due to strong performance.
  • Risk Tolerance: This portfolio is tailored for a high-risk investor. Be prepared for significant market fluctuations and maintain a long-term perspective.
  • Crypto Exposure: The 10% crypto allocation offers high-growth potential but comes with extreme volatility. This is a speculative part of your portfolio; only invest what you are comfortable losing.
