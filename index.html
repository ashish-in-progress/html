Tips and Important Notes

Diversification is Key

While this portfolio is high-risk, it still utilizes diversification across stocks, mutual funds, crypto, and traditional assets like gold. This helps mitigate concentration risk, though overall volatility remains high due to aggressive allocation.

Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs)

Continuing your monthly SIP of ₹1500 helps average out your purchase cost over time (Rupee Cost Averaging), reducing the impact of market volatility and fostering disciplined investing. Consistency is vital for long-term growth.

Hedging with Gold/FD

The small allocation to Gold and Fixed Deposits acts as a partial hedge against extreme market downturns in equity and crypto, providing a minimal safety net while maintaining the high-risk profile.

Review Regularly

Given the high-risk nature and dynamic market conditions, it's advisable to review your portfolio at least quarterly. Rebalance if necessary to maintain your desired asset allocation and ensure it still aligns with your risk tolerance and goals.
