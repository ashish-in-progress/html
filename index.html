What‑If Analysis & Scenarios

Scenario outcomes (₹)

Assumptions for illustration: Base 1y: Stocks +12%, MFs +10% (blend), FDs +7%. Mild downturn: Stocks −10%, MFs −7%, FDs +7%. Severe crash: Stocks −30%, MFs −20%, FDs +7%.

Risk Corner: Crash Impact

  • Equity exposure: ~30% direct stocks + ~60% of MF bucket in equity ≈ total equity 60% of portfolio.
  • Severe crash test (illustrative): If equities −30% and debt MF −7% while FDs earn +7%, portfolio drawdown ≈ −17% to −19% from ₹2,000 (to ~₹1,650–₹1,620).
  • Volatility: Expect short‑term swings; rebalancing annually back to 30/50/20 helps manage risk.
  • Buffers: FD/debt allocation and gold sleeve inside MFs can soften equity shocks.
  • Actionables: Keep 3–6 months expenses separate as emergency fund; avoid selling quality during panic.
Rebalance: Annual or if weights deviate by >5% Horizon: 5+ years for equity portion
