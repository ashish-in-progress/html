Tips & Notes for a Robust Portfolio

Diversification is Key

Ensure your portfolio isn't overly concentrated in one asset class. A mix of equities, debt, and gold helps mitigate risks and capture opportunities across different market cycles.

Power of SIPs

Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) help in rupee cost averaging, reducing the impact of market volatility and building wealth consistently over the long term.

Hedge Against Inflation

Assets like Gold ETFs or inflation-indexed bonds can act as a hedge against inflation, preserving your purchasing power when prices rise.

Regular Rebalancing

Periodically review and adjust your portfolio allocation to bring it back to your target weights. This helps in booking profits and cutting losses.
