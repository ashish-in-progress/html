Important Tips & Notes

  • Diversification: While this portfolio has crypto, consider other asset classes like Gold ETFs, REITs, or international equities for broader diversification based on your risk tolerance.
  • Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs): Regularly investing through SIPs can help average out purchase costs and build wealth over time, especially in volatile markets.
  • Hedges: Explore hedging strategies to protect against significant downturns. This could include a small allocation to gold, debt, or inverse ETFs during uncertain times.
  • Rebalancing: Periodically review and rebalance your portfolio to maintain your target asset allocation and risk level.
  • Market Volatility: Cryptocurrencies are highly volatile. Invest only what you can afford to lose.
  • Long-Term Horizon: This portfolio is aggressive. A long-term investment horizon (3+ years) is crucial to ride out market fluctuations and realize potential returns.
