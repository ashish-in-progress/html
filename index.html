Mild Market Crash

Severe Market Crash

Tips & Notes

Diversification

Diversifying your investments across different asset classes (stocks, crypto, mutual funds, gold, bonds) helps to mitigate risk. When one asset class performs poorly, another might perform well, balancing your overall portfolio.

Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs)

SIPs are a great way to invest regularly, reducing the impact of market volatility through rupee-cost averaging. Consistent, disciplined investing over time can lead to significant wealth creation.

Hedging Strategies

Consider hedging your portfolio against inflation or market downturns. Assets like Gold ETFs or certain types of bonds can act as hedges, providing stability when other investments are under pressure.

Review Regularly

Markets change constantly. It's crucial to review your portfolio periodically (e.g., quarterly or annually) to rebalance your asset allocation, adjust to new market conditions, and ensure it still aligns with your financial goals.
