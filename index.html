Tips and Notes for Your Portfolio

  • Diversification: Your portfolio includes a mix of stocks, mutual funds, gold, and fixed deposits. This diversification is crucial to manage risk and provide stability during market fluctuations.
  • SIPs (Systematic Investment Plans): Consider regular SIPs into your selected mutual funds. This strategy helps average out your purchase cost and builds wealth over the long term, reducing the impact of market volatility.
  • Hedges: Gold serves as an excellent hedge against inflation and market downturns. Maintaining a portion in gold (15% in your case) provides a cushion during economic uncertainties.
  • Long-Term Vision: Investments, especially in equities, perform best over the long term. Avoid frequent trading based on short-term market noise. Stick to your 3-year plan and review annually.
  • Emergency Fund: Ensure you have a separate emergency fund covering 6-12 months of expenses before investing aggressively. Fixed deposits serve as a safe, accessible component of this.
